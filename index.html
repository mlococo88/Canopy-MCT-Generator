Column wind pressure
Fascia lateral wind = fascia depth × pressure × tributary length, as a point load at the beam end. Column wind = pressure × projected column width, as a line load over the height. In Calculate mode each pressure is derived as a flat-sided member per ASCE 7-16 §29.4 (Fig. 29.4-1, normal to face) by aspect ratio.
Windscreen
Screen wind pressure
Lateral screen wind applied as a uniform load over the screen height, on one column line. In Calculate mode the pressure is derived as a solid sign per ASCE 7-16 §29.3 (Fig 29.3-1 C_f from the screen's B/s and s/h), reduced for openings via §29.3.1 using the percent-open you enter.
